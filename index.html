Why Invest in Different Assets?

Gold

Gold is a safe asset and protects against inflation and market crashes.

Stocks

Stocks offer high returns over long term but involve moderate risk.

Crypto

Crypto has very high return potential but also very high risk.

Fixed Deposit (FD)

FD is low risk and provides stable but lower returns.

💡 Investment Tips for Beginners

  • Diversify your portfolio: Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Spread your money across different assets to minimize risk.
  • Start early: The power of compound interest works best when you give your investments time to grow.
  • Understand your risk tolerance: Invest in assets that match your comfort level with risk and your financial goals.
  • Avoid emotional decisions: Market fluctuations are normal. Stick to your long-term plan instead of reacting to short-term market noise.
